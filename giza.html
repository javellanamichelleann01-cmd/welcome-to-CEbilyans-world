PYRAMID OF GIZA

A recipe over 4,500 years old. As the only surviving wonder of the ancient world, it showcases the pinnacle of Egyptian structural engineering and logistics, built using over 2 million limestone blocks.

Chef's Note: The precision is elite—the base is level within just 15 millimeters across 13 acres! Source: Lehner, M. (1997). The complete pyramids. Thames & Hudson.
